TIP MANAGEMENT
FAIR TIPS.
NO SPREADSHEETS.
FireSync's tip pool engine is built around a simple principle: staff should earn based on the time and role they put in — not the luck of which table left a big tip. Choose the method that fits your operation.
★ RECOMMENDED
Weighted Hours
Hours worked × role weight ÷ total weighted hours. Rewards time on floor, not table luck. Harder to game. Better team culture. Automatically adjusts for day-part pools.
TOAST-COMPATIBLE
Sales % by Category
Distribute based on food, alcohol, or merch sales percentages. Familiar to teams migrating from Toast. Works well in bar-heavy operations.
SIMPLE
Equal by Hours
Everyone earns the same per-hour rate. Flat, transparent, and easy to explain. Best for small teams or operations where everyone wears multiple hats.
H.R.1 Ready (2025–2028): Under the Tax Cuts and Jobs Act extension, tip income is federally tax-exempt. FireSync tracks gross tip earnings per employee per shift — screenshot-ready for tax records.
